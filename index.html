who we are

Vulcan Circle is a subscription membership providing firearm optics through two unique services.

Our "rent to rotate" service provides members access to three different armory types in which members select optics on a three-month rotation, allowing them to experience different optics before purchasing.

Likewise, under our "rent to purchase" option, members can purchase a single optic to be paid off over an 8 or 12 month period.

Both services are granted access to extraordinary incentives like our rewards program, Vulcan Honors. It's our goal to provide members unmatched opportunity in how they experience firearm accessories. If that sounds like something you want to be apart of, join The Circle today!

our rewards program

Vulcan Honors is the Circle’s rewards program. Members accumulate points monthly which they can use to purchase optics, accessories, partner products and more found on the "eVulcan Honors" page. Additionally, members are supplemented with a point bonus when optics are returned in good condition.

armory

three armory types to meet your needs.

legionary

The Legionary is the Circle’s entry-level armory. Borrowing the name from the Latin term, legionarius, a legionary was a Roman infantryman, responsible for fighting on the forefront of battle. The Legionary Armory gives you access to optics like the Aimpoint T2, the Trijicon ACOG and more.

centurion

The Centurion Armory stands as the second tier in our lineup. The armory’s namesake comes from the Centurion soldier, a Roman military officer responsible for the well-being and combat effectiveness of the legionaries. This armory provides members access to optics such as the Elcan Specter, any optic within the Legionary Armory and more.

tribune

Tribune, or in Latin, Tribunus, was the officer title given to the second-in-command of a Roman legion. Additionally, the rank was often bestowed to those in line for a seat in Rome’s Senate; a highly-esteemed position in the Roman Empire. The Tribune Armory is Vulcan Circle’s premier membership program, providing members access to optics from Tangent Theta, U.S. Optics’ B25 series, any optic in the preceding armory types, and more.
